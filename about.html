About Us

Learn more about 2SMS Company Limited

Company Overview

2SMS Company Limited is a leading distributor of motor oils and lubricants based in Temeke, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Located near Alhikima Girls Secondary School, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses and individuals seeking quality lubricant products.

With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we serve clients across Tanzania, providing premium products and reliable service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality motor oils and lubricants to customers across Tanzania, ensuring reliable supply, competitive pricing, and exceptional service that meets the evolving needs of the automotive and industrial sectors.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred lubricant distributor in Tanzania, recognized for our product quality, nationwide reach, and unwavering commitment to customer success.

Core Values

  • Quality — We deliver only premium, genuine products
  • Reliability — Consistent supply and on-time delivery
  • Integrity — Honest business practices and transparency
  • Customer Focus — Your success is our priority
  • Innovation — Continuously improving our services
